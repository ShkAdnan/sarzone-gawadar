What is Appeal Medical Board and what is the process?
Candidate who apply against the decision of their Medical, they should forwarded a request to Recruitment Directorate for arranging an Appeal Medical Board to reassess the verdict of their Medical Unfitness. Recruitment Directorate will further process the case with Medical Directorate for arranging their Appeal Medical Board. Appeal Medical Board will be held at CMH Rawalpindi only.
I am not satisfied with final Medical result as being declared UNFIT. How can I challenge the decision?
You may apply for Appeal Medical Board. You have to appear before a team of medical officers who will give final decision regarding medical fitness or otherwise. Further details may be asked by contacting Recruitment Directorate on 051-20062370-74.
I belong to Hyderabad, but at present, I am in Lahore, may I apply for sailor in PN Recruitment and Selection Centre in Lahore?
Yes, you can apply at the nearest Recruitment and Selection Centre in any where in Pakistan.
I am under age by only 10 days. Can I apply for the sailor?
No, you are not eligible for the current batch but in the next batch you may apply. You may contact your nearest PN Recruitment and Selection Centre for further guidance.
